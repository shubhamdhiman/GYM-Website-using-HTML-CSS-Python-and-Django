About Us

We Fireup helping people live longer, happier and healthier lives for over 10 years.

Fireup was founded in 2011 as a family owned and operated business specialising in supplying high-quality gym equipment at great prices. For more than 10 years helping customers reach their fitness goals has been at the heart of what we do and why we do it!

Fireup has a growing 60+ awesome superstars and we owe our success to each and every of them. We know that building a positive culture is incredibly important. We believe in encouraging, supporting, challenging, learning and growing to be the best! Our flexible working solutions, gym rebates and educational opportunities create a positive work/life balance for all our employees.
